CashContract

public final class CashContract

The contract between the cash management provider and applications. Contains definitions for the supported URIs and columns.

Cash events are recorded locally on device only and not synced between devices or sent to the cloud. This is a read-only contract. Cash events are created when cash payments or refunds occur. To manually record cash events use com.clover.sdk.v3.cash.CashEvents.
